Planned Giving

Make Angel Foundation™ a part of your lasting legacy by designating us as a beneficiary in your will, estate plan, or beneficiary designation. Consult your financial advisor or estate planning attorney to explore legacy giving.

Workplace Giving

If your company participates in a workplace giving program, you can make monthly tax-deductible contributions to Angel Foundation™. Angel Foundation’s™ EIN number is 41-1990883.

Employer Matching Gifts Program

Many employers will match employee donations to a charity. Check with your HR department to see if your company offers this benefit.
