Dmitriy & Anna's Birkie 30 KM

heading-has-long-words: text_html: >-

This year, on February 20, me and Anna will once again be participating in the Birkie 30 km ski race. American Birkibeiner, or Birkie, is the largest cross-country ski race in the US. As part of this effort, we hope to raise funds to support the Angel Foundation.

Angel Foundation provides vital relief to adults with cancer and their loved ones through financial assistance, education, and emotional support. Nearly every family in the U.S. is touched by cancer in one way or another, and the disease — along with its treatment — places an enormous emotional and financial burden on those affected.
