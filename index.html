What is an Angel Pack™?

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: text: description_html: >-

Angel Packs™ are age-appropriate and therapeutic kits for kids who have a loved one with cancer. Packs are free to all Minnesota residents in active treatment for cancer or available for purchase on our store site or Etsy. Click the buttons below to apply for a free pack or gift a pack to give the gift of comfort to a loved one impacted by cancer.

Angel's Impact

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: 58k text: Served Since 2001 - featured: 250+ text: Angel Packs™ Provided Annually - featured: $15 text: Million in Financial Aid description_html: >-

Founded in 2001, Angel Foundation™ began by providing financial assistance to cancer patients struggling to cover basic, nonmedical needs. Since then, we have expanded our offerings to include social events for families, therapeutic kits to help kids understand cancer and its treatment, educational programs, financial guidance, resource navigation, and more. Our goal is to be the premiere resource for cancer patients in Minnesota.
