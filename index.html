What is an Angel Pack™?

facts-loop: - number: text: description_html: >-

Angel Packs™ are age-appropriate and therapeutic kits for kids who have a loved one with cancer. Packs are free to all Minnesota residents in active treatment for cancer or available for purchase on our store site or Etsy. Click the “Gift a Pack” button below to give the gift of comfort to a loved one impacted by cancer, or click "Apply" to apply for a free pack.
