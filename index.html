What is an Angel Pack™?

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: text: description_html: >-

Angel Packs™ are age-appropriate, therapeutic kits for kids who have an adult loved one with cancer. Packs are free to all Minnesota residents in active cancer treatment or available for purchase on our store or Etsy. Click “Gift a Pack” to send comfort to a loved one or click "Apply" to apply for a free pack.

Angel's Impact

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: 58K text: Lives Touched - featured: $15M text: in Financial Aid - featured: 4K text: Served Annually description_html: >-

Founded in 2001, Angel Foundation™ began by providing financial assistance to cancer patients for basic, nonmedical needs. We have since expanded to offer resource navigation, educational programs, financial guidance, social events for families, and therapeutic kits to help kids understand cancer and its treatment and more. Our goal is to be the premiere resource for cancer patients in Minnesota.
