Angel Gala

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Our largest fundraiser of the year gathers 1,000 members of the cancer community for a party with a purpose at The Depot Minneapolis. Guests enjoy a cocktail reception, silent auction, entertainment, and a dinner program featuring patient stories and the presentation of our annual Founder’s Award. This magical evening raises more than $1 million dollars to support cancer patients and their families.

Registration will open in October of 2025.

Lives We Touch Luncheon

heading-has-long-words: true description_html: >- Angel Foundation™ welcomes those who have been impacted by cancer to learn more about our mission to support adult cancer patients across Minnesota. Guests enjoy an artisan pop-up market, a complimentary lunch, and a heartwarming program highlighting the lives touched by our services, and an opportunity to support our vital programs.

Registration will open in March of 2026.

Hope in Motion

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Hope in Motion is an annual 5K run and family walk, presented by Minnesota Oncology on National Cancer Survivors' Day. Participants of all ages and abilities gather at Veterans Park in Richfield, Minnesota to celebrate survivorship. The event features a 5K run, 1-mile family walk, kids dash, and survivor celebration. Participants are encouraged to fundraise for Angel Foundation™ by reaching out to their networks (friends, family, colleagues) for donations.

Registration will open in March of 2026.

Angel & Eagles Golf Classic

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Each July, Angel Foundation™ invites golf enthusiasts to play the premier course at Minnesota Valley Country Club. Golfers enjoy a fun day of golf while supporting cancer patients across Minnesota in a variety of ways: purchasing mulligans, hole-in-one and putting contests, a silent auction, raffles, corporate sponsorships, and individual gifts.
