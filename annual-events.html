Angel Gala

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Our largest fundraiser of the year gathers 1,000 members of the cancer community for a party with a purpose at The Depot Minneapolis. Guests enjoy a cocktail reception, silent auction, entertainment, and a dinner program featuring patient stories and the presentation of our annual Founder’s Award. This magical evening raises more than $1 million dollars to support cancer patients and their families.

Registration will open in October.

Lives We Touch Luncheon

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Angel Foundation™ welcomes those who have been impacted by cancer to learn more about our mission to support adult cancer patients across Minnesota. Guests enjoy an artisan pop-up market, a complimentary lunch, and heartwarming program highlighting the lives touched by our services, and an opportunity to support our vital programs.

Registration will open in March of 2026.

Hope in Motion

heading-has-long-words: description_html: >- Hope in Motion is an annual 5K run and family walk, presented by Minnesota Oncology on National Cancer Survivors' Day. Participants of all ages and abilities gather at Veterans Park in Richfield, Minnesota to celebrate survivorship. The event features a 5K run, 1-mile family walk, kids dash, and survivor celebration.
