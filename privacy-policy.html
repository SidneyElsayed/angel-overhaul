Privacy Policy

text_html: >-

We will not sell, share or trade our donors’ names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations online or offline, on any Platform (‘Platform’, includes the Angel Foundation™ website and mobile applications), or by electronic, written, or oral communications. To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, your information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.
