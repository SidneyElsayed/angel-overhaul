We invite you to join us at the Lives We Touch Luncheon in Duluth, an upcoming Angel Foundation™ fundraiser to benefit adult cancer patients across northern Minnesota. The luncheon takes place on Wednesday, August 5, 2026 at Greysolon by Black Woods in Duluth. Lunch is complimentary, and it is a terrific opportunity for people to learn more about Angel Foundation’s™ expanding presence in northern Minnesota and the hundreds of patients we have served here. The heartwarming program will feature a local patient who has benefitted from our resources and leaders making an impact in our cancer care community.

Please RSVP by July 22 via email to danderson@mnangel.org.
