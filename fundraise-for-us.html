- question: 'How do I submit my funds from the event to Angel Foundation™? ' answer_html: >-

You can submit the funds raised to Angel Foundation™ via check, ACH transfer, or through a custom donation page made for your event. Contact us for more details.

- question: >- Do third-party fundraisers need to raise a certain amount to support Angel Foundation’s™ mission? answer_html: >-

Angel Foundation™ welcomes third-party fundraisers of all sizes and formats. No event is too big or too small to make an impact on the patients that we serve. While we recommend setting a fundraising goal to motivate participants and donors, there is no required minimum donation.

- question: 'Can I designate my gift to support a specific program? ' answer_html: >-

If you would like your gift to support one of our four program areas, Emergency Financial Assistance, Financial Cancer Care, Adult & Family Programs, or Angel Packs™, please note that in the memo line of your check or the online form.
