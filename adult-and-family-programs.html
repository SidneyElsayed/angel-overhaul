To be eligible to participate in Adult & Family Programs, you must be:

  • A cancer survivor, current cancer patient, or a family member of someone going through cancer.

  • Living in or being treated in our service area (Anoka, Carver, Chisago, Dakota, Hennepin, Isanti, Olmsted, Ramsey, Scott, Sherburne, St. Louis, Washington, or Wright Counties in Minnesota and Douglas and St. Croix Counties in Wisconsin.) We request that families attend Adult & Family Programs in the metro area where they reside (Twin Cities, Duluth, or Rochester.)

- question: I do not remember if I completed an application. How can I check? answer_html: >-

Log in to your portal here. If you have not completed an application, you will see a box that says “+ Get Started.” If you have completed an application, the box will say your name with “Completed” or “Approved” under it.

- question: >- I completed an application to attend an event in the past. Do I need to complete another one for the next event? answer_html: >-

No. One application covers all Adult & Family Programs. However, a separate registration form is required for each Making Memories or Educational Program. Instructions to complete registration for an Adult & Family Programs event are available here.

- question: I’m trying to register for an event and am having trouble. Help! answer_html: >-

Please follow the process described here. If you still need help, call 612-627-9000 or email programs@mnangel.org.

- question: >- If my family visits an area where a Making Memories event is planned, but we don’t live nearby, can we still attend? answer_html: >-

A key goal of our Adult & Family programs is to help participants connect with their local cancer communities. We ask that you only attend events in the area where you live or receive treatment (e.g., Duluth events are for St. Louis County residents or those treated in Duluth). Camp Angel, Camp Snow Angel, and virtual events are open to all, regardless of location.
