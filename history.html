Our Founders

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: text: description_html: >-

Mark and Margie Sborov met in high school through their friend, Jim, who died of leukemia at 16. Jim's death profoundly affected them both, leading Mark to become an Oncologist and Margie a Patient Navigator. In 2001, they founded Angel Foundation™ to help cancer patients with immediate nonmedical expenses. Since then, Angel Foundation™ has provided over $15 million in direct financial assistance and served 58,000 cancer patients.
