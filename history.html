Our Founders

heading-has-long-words: facts-loop: - featured: text: description_html: >-

Mark and Margie Sborov met in high school through their friend, Jim, who died of leukemia at 16 years old. Jim's death profoundly affected Mark and Margie, who both went on to work in the field of oncology, Mark as an Oncologist and Margie as a Patient Navigator. In 2001, they founded Angel Foundation™ to help cancer patients with immediate nonmedical expenses. Since then, Angel Foundation™ has provided over $15 Million in direct financial assistance and served 58,000 cancer patients.
